# Braces & Clear Aligners at One Smile Dental Care

A straight, well-aligned smile is not only beautiful—it is also essential for healthy teeth, functional chewing, and overall wellness. At One Smile Dental Care, we provide comprehensive orthodontic care for children, teens, and adults. Whether you want traditional metal braces, discreet ceramic braces, or modern clear aligners, we design custom treatment plans to help you achieve your goals.

## Our Orthodontic Solutions:

### 1. Clear Aligners (Clear Trays)
Clear aligners are the modern, discreet alternative to metal braces. We use custom-crafted, clear, BPA-free plastic aligner trays that gently shift your teeth into position.
- **Virtually Invisible:** No one will notice you are straightening your teeth.
- **Removable:** Take them out to eat, brush, and floss. No dietary restrictions!
- **Comfortable:** Smooth edges that won't irritate your cheeks or lips.

### 2. Aesthetic Ceramic Braces
Ceramic braces function just like metal braces, but the brackets are made of clear or tooth-colored ceramic material, making them blend in with your natural teeth.

### 3. Traditional Metal Braces
Reliable, durable, and highly effective for correcting even the most complex bite and alignment issues.
