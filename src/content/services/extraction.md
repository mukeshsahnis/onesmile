# Safe & Gentle Tooth Extractions

At One Smile Dental Care, our primary goal is always to preserve your natural teeth. However, there are circumstances where saving a tooth is not possible or not recommended for your overall oral health. In these cases, we perform **extractions** with the utmost gentleness, care, and sterilization.

## Reasons for Tooth Extraction:
1. **Severe Decay or Infection:** When decay reaches deep into the tooth and cannot be repaired with a root canal.
2. **Advanced Gum Disease:** When periodontitis has caused significant bone loss, making the tooth loose and unsupported.
3. **Impacted Wisdom Teeth:** Wisdom teeth (third molars) often fail to erupt properly, becoming trapped in the bone or gums, which causes pain, swelling, and damage to adjacent molars.
4. **Orthodontic Crowding:** Sometimes, teeth must be removed to create space for braces or aligners to align the remaining teeth.
5. **Fractured Teeth:** Vertically cracked teeth or fractures that extend deep below the gum line cannot be restored and must be removed.
