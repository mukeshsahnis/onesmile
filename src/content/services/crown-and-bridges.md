# Precision Crowns & Bridges at One Smile Dental Care

When teeth are weakened by decay, fractures, or root canal therapy, or when teeth are missing altogether, **crowns and bridges** are the go-to solutions to restore your smile’s function and beauty. At One Smile Dental Care, we use high-quality, biocompatible materials like Zirconia, E-Max, and porcelain-fused-to-metal (PFM) to create restorations that match your natural smile seamlessly.

## What is a Dental Crown?

Often called a "cap", a crown covers the entire visible portion of a compromised tooth down to the gum line. It restores the tooth's shape, size, strength, and appearance. Crowns are critical after a root canal or for teeth with large fillings that lack structural integrity.

## What is a Dental Bridge?

A bridge is used to replace one or more missing teeth. It consists of two or more crowns for the anchoring teeth (abutment teeth) on either side of the space, with a false tooth (pontic) suspended in between.
