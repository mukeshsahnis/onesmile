# Premium Dentures at One Smile Dental Care

Tooth loss can significantly affect your diet, speech, and self-confidence. At One Smile Dental Care, we craft **custom dentures** that are designed to fit the unique shape of your gums, restoring a natural look and comfortable chewing function.

We offer modern, lightweight, and aesthetic materials, moving away from bulky, uncomfortable traditional designs.

## Our Denture Options:

### 1. Flexible Partial Dentures
Made from modern, biocompatible thermoplastic material, these dentures are thin, lightweight, and completely metal-free. They blend in invisibly with your gums and do not put stress on neighboring teeth.

### 2. Complete Dentures
For patients missing all teeth on their upper or lower arch. We meticulously shape these to restore your facial profile and lip support.

### 3. Implant-Supported Overdentures
For the ultimate stability, we place a few dental implants that securely lock your dentures in place. This provides unparalleled chewing comfort and eliminates the fear of dentures falling out while eating or speaking.
