# Porcelain & Composite Veneers

If you are looking for a dramatic yet natural-looking smile transformation, **porcelain or composite veneers** are one of the most effective treatments available. Veneers are wafer-thin, custom-made shells designed to cover the front surface of your teeth, instantly concealing cosmetic imperfections.

At One Smile Dental Care, we meticulously design and place veneers that blend seamlessly with your facial features, giving you a symmetrical, bright, and flawless smile.

## Cosmetic Concerns Veneers Can Address:

- **Severe Discoloration:** Stains that cannot be removed by bleaching (such as internal staining from tetracycline or trauma).
- **Chipped or Worn Teeth:** Restoring teeth that have minor chips, fractures, or have been worn down from grinding.
- **Crooked or Misaligned Teeth:** Correcting minor overlaps or tilts without the need for braces (often referred to as 'instant orthodontics').
- **Gaps & Spacing:** Closing unsightly gaps between front teeth.
