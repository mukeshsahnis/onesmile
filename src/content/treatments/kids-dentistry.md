# Pediatric & Kids Dentistry

At One Smile Dental Care, we love helping children develop beautiful, healthy smiles. Our team understands that a child's early experiences at the dental clinic shape their attitude toward oral health for the rest of their life. That’s why we have created a warm, friendly, and completely non-threatening environment for our youngest patients.

## Our Pediatric Treatments Include:

- **Gentle Oral Exams:** Fun checkups to count your child's teeth and check on gum health.
- **Fluoride Treatments:** A simple, quick varnish application that strengthens developing enamel and halts early decay.
- **Dental Sealants:** Safely sealing the deep fissures of permanent molars to act as a barrier against cavity-causing bacteria.
- **Habit Counseling:** Friendly guidance to help children stop thumb-sucking or tongue-thrusting, preventing skeletal issues.
- **Space Maintainers:** Custom appliances that keep adjacent teeth from shifting if a baby tooth is lost prematurely.
