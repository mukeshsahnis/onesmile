# Preventive Dentistry at One Smile Dental Care

The best dental treatment is the one you never need. **Preventive care** is the foundation of lifetime oral health. At One Smile Dental Care, we focus on helping you maintain a healthy mouth through routine checkups, professional cleanings (scaling and polishing), and proactive education.

## What is Included in Preventive Care?

1. **Comprehensive Dental Exams:** A thorough look at your teeth, gums, and oral cavity to identify signs of decay, wear, or oral disease.
2. **Professional Scaling and Polishing:** Using ultrasonic instruments to gently vibrated away hard tartar deposits (calculus) that cannot be removed by brushing alone, followed by polishing to remove stains.
3. **Digital Dental X-Rays:** Helping us spot decay between teeth, bone loss, or infections that are invisible to the naked eye.
4. **Patient Education:** Tailored brushing and flossing advice specific to your mouth’s unique structure.
