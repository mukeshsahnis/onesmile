# Professional Teeth Whitening Treatments

A bright, white smile is associated with youth, health, and vitality. Over time, factors like diet (coffee, tea, soda, wine), tobacco use, certain medications, and natural aging can cause teeth to lose their luster and become yellow or stained.

At One Smile Dental Care, we offer professional **teeth whitening** treatments that safely and quickly lift stains, giving you a sparkling, confident smile in just a single appointment.

## Why Professional Whitening Over Store-Bought Kits?

While drugstore whitening strips and toothpaste seem convenient, they often yield uneven results and can cause gum irritation or enamel damage due to ill-fitting trays and abrasive chemicals.

- **Fast & Powerful:** In-office laser whitening uses high-concentration whitening gels activated by a specialized light to deliver dramatic results in just one hour.
- **Customized & Even:** We customize the application to ensure even shade coverage across all teeth.
- **Gum Protection:** We isolate your lips and gums with special barriers so the whitening agent only touches your teeth, preventing chemical burns.
